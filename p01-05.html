Product Assembly Manual: Flower

This product is a core apparatus responsible for plant reproduction. Assemble it in the prescribed order below.

Step 1. Install the base and protective unit

  • Component Sepal
  • Purpose Protects and supports the overall structure while internal components are assembled.
  • Method Arrange the sepal in a circular formation at the base and secure it in place.

Step 2. Attach the attraction outer panels

  • Component Petal
  • Purpose Attracts external agents and encloses the internal reproductive components.
  • Method Fix petals around the centre until the corolla is formed.

Step 3. Assemble the male reproductive module

  • Component Stamen
  • Purpose Produces and releases pollen.
  • Method Place the stamen set in the centre above the petals.

Step 4. Seat the central control unit

  • Component Pistil
  • Purpose Receives pollen and forms seeds as the final destination of fertilisation.
  • Method Fix the pistil vertically at the exact centre. Verify that all components are firmly connected.
